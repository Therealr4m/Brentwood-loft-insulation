Upgrading Existing Insulation the Correct Way

We don’t simply add material blindly. We measure existing depth, check airflow at the eaves and ensure ventilation isn’t compromised before installing additional mineral wool to reach the appropriate standard.

Proper layering ensures insulation performs effectively long term without compression or airflow restriction.

Loft Insulation Top-Up Questions in Brentwood

How do I know if I need a loft insulation top-up?

If your insulation measures below 270mm but is otherwise dry and in good condition, a top-up is often the most suitable option.

Is a top-up better than full replacement?

If existing insulation is damaged, damp or poorly fitted, replacement may be necessary. But where materials are stable, topping up is typically more efficient and cost-effective.

How long does a loft insulation top-up take?

Most standard Brentwood loft top-ups can be completed within a single day depending on loft size and accessibility.
