Loft Insulation for Victorian & Edwardian Homes

Chelmsford features large areas of Victorian and Edwardian housing, particularly in central residential streets and established neighbourhoods across CM1 and CM2. These character properties often have generous roof voids but limited original insulation.

Older homes may contain outdated materials or insufficient insulation depth, allowing significant heat to escape through the loft space. Carefully upgrading these properties requires attention to ventilation and moisture control to preserve the integrity of the roof structure.

Springfield & Great Baddow Loft Insulation Upgrades

Residential estates in areas such as Springfield and Great Baddow include a mixture of mid-century family homes and more recent developments. Many of these properties benefit from insulation upgrades that bring them in line with current efficiency standards.

Modern Developments & PIR Insulation Improvements

Chelmsford has seen substantial new-build expansion in recent years. While modern homes are typically insulated to building regulation standards, some property owners choose to upgrade further using high-performance rigid PIR insulation to maximise energy efficiency.

PIR boards are particularly effective in certain roof constructions where space is limited but improved thermal resistance is desired. We assess suitability carefully to ensure compatibility with the property’s structure and ventilation system.
